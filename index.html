About Tachyon

Carefully thought of in the dawn of 2015 with the primary motive of promoting cutting-edge technology encouraging innovation and fostering creativity,Tachyon 2019 has been initiated by the computer society of India JUET chapter and the CSE department to bring out the best from the brains of our university. Tachyon,from Greek takhus meaning swift, refers to a hypothetical subatomic particle that has a speed greater than the speed of light. It represents the nature of thinking machine. Our mission,through this series,is not only to inspire budding engineers to explore, discover and connect with the endless world of renowned personalities and myriad possibilities, but to go beyond limits (faster than light, maybe) and find solutions to their banal yet significant problems. Tachyon is an endeavor to provide an outstanding platform for the students to showcase their talent and skillsets in fiercest of competitions. Tachyon strives for one and all to get inspired and make an effort to change the world.

Event by Computer Society of India(CSI)
