Quint Hurdle

It is a clue-based riddle that the entire team must solve to come out as a winner. Rather than using a computer screen, you have to move out and use all the available clues to find the solution in the real world to get to the next round.

Each team starts with a riddle in their hands which when decoded perfectly, will point to a location in the university campus, there you will find a clue to the next riddle. Following the clue to the end of the line, you will reach to the finishing point. The first team to solve all the puzzles wins the game.

Cryptic Hunt

If you think you can trigger your mind to guess the passwords easily and is good at problem solving as it is a major aspect of it.This game is basically based on web encryption. The person has to think like a hacker to find the passwords of the next webpage. The individual who is able to guess maximum passwords wins the game.
